Our Foundation

The Story Behind the Scent

For over three generations, PERFUME has championed the art of fragrance, blending heritage techniques with modern sustainability to craft timeless luxuries.

A Heritage of Excellence

Founded in 1952 by master perfumer Antoine Dubois, our house was built on a single, unwavering principle: that true luxury lies in patience and precision. We reject synthetic shortcuts, instead dedicating ourselves to the careful extraction and blending of the world's most exquisite natural ingredients. This commitment ensures every bottle holds a depth and complexity that machine-made scents simply cannot replicate.

"The finest scent is not simply made; it is grown, curated, and finally, perfected." - Antoine Dubois

Our Philosophy: Sustainability & Sourcing

Ethical Sourcing

We partner directly with small farms globally, ensuring fair trade practices and sustainable harvesting techniques for rare ingredients like Haitian Vetiver and Bulgarian Rose.

Artisanal Blending

Every batch is hand-blended and aged in our private cellars, a process that can take months, allowing the notes to marry and develop their full, rich character.

Minimalist Packaging

Our iconic bottles are reusable and our packaging is 100% biodegradable, reflecting our commitment to the planet as much as to quality fragrance.
